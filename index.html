Location-Based Narratives

Join a location based, storytelling game in the Athens Trigono area. Stories of local craftsman are weaved together with urban tales of modern Athens. In order for you to discover them, you only need to walk around the city streets and scan the QR codes you find in your way.

About

We are an award winning startup in the Cultural & Creative Industry participating in the 2nd cohort of Athens Digital Lab, the 1st municipal early-stage tech incubator for ”smart cities” digital solutions. We are also one of the winning teams of the airport innovation challenge Digital Gate III, sponsored by the Athens International Airport (AIA) with the support of Athens Center for Entrepreneurship and Innovation (ACEin).
